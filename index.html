About VertiDog

A new Miami food-truck concept built around vertical hot dogs served in toasted buns with premium sausages, bold sauces, and serious curb appeal.

The hot dog reimagined.

VertiDog takes the classic hot dog and flips the experience upright. The bun is toasted, the sausage sits vertically, and the toppings stay inside the bite instead of falling everywhere.

It is fast, photogenic, easy to eat, and built for Miami crowds — lunch rushes, beach traffic, events, late-night snacks, pop-ups, and delivery.
